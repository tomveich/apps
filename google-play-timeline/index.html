An Unresolved Google Play Support Case: A Developer's Timeline

In July 2024, my Google Play Developer account was terminated due to an incorrect association with another account. After a difficult, year-long appeal process, my account was successfully reinstated in March 2025. Unfortunately, a cascade of automated system errors on the day of reinstatement left all of my apps in a "Suspended" state, where they have been stuck for over five months, rendering them completely un-updateable. This document provides a clear, evidence-backed timeline of these events in an effort to find a resolution.

Event 1: The Reinstatement

 Mar 7, 2025, 2:41 AM  UTC + 2

 My Google Play Developer account is officially reinstated after a successful, year-long appeal. 

 < 5 hours, 28 minutes passed >

 Mar 7, 2025, 8:09 AM  UTC + 2
 Less than 6 hours later, I receive the first of three automated emails. My reinstated account is now "Restricted" due to an "Identity verification" issue.  

 < 6 minutes passed > 

 Mar 7, 2025, 8:15 AM  UTC + 2

 < 2 hours, 31 minutes passed>

Event 3: The Immediate Fix

 Mar 7, 2025, 10:47:30 AM  UTC + 2

 The identity issue is suddenly resolved. I receive an email confirming: "Your identity has been verified." This happened less than 3 hours after the problem started.  

 < 5 months, 8 days, 12 hours, 12 minutes passed > 

Event 4: The Lingering Consequence (5+ Months Later)

My developer account is verified and in good standing. However, all of my apps remain incorrectly "Suspended" - a status triggered by the temporary ID flag that was fixed on the same day. The Play Console interface remains completely locked. 






As of August 2025, my developer account is fully verified and in good standing, yet my apps remain inaccessible due to the administrative error that occurred on March 7th. The standard support channels have acknowledged the issue but have been unable to resolve it, as the case appears to require a manual correction that front-line support cannot perform. My objective is simple: to have the incorrect "Suspended" status lifted from my apps so that I may resume development, update them to meet all current policies, and make them available to users once more.
