# Privacy policy

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes the data practices of the Blind Find game ("App," "We," "Us," or "Our").

Blind Find is completely ad-free and respects your privacy.

1. Information Collection

The game does not collect any information from your device or about your usage. This includes but is not limited to:

- Device identifiers
- Location data
- Contact information
- App usage data

2. Information Sharing

We do not share any information with third-party service providers or any other organizations.

3. Security

While the game itself does not collect any data, we recommend you familiarize yourself with the data security practices of your device and any app store you use to download Fidget.

4. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this Policy at any time. The updated Policy will be available within the App and on the App's listing on the app store.

5. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this Policy, please refer to our contact information available on the game's listing on the app store.
